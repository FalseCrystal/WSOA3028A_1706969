While “The Message is the Medium” comes from an older time, addressing older technologies, it is interesting to relate the messages it conveyed to present day technology and society.

One of its core messages is how the medium tends to be overshadowed by the content it conveys, both from a value and moral point of view. What must be considered is that while the morality and value of technology is decided by its users, different technologies were made for specific purposes that assists humans in various activities. The convenience it provides often encourages how often the medium is used for their specific functions which shows in a current society regarding communication and social media. Likewise, the development of weapons has made it all the easier for people to defend themselves and just as easily harm others.

The main concern becomes apparent in how we have become numb to the weight of our actions that have been made more conveniently doable.

By Ryan Ka Kit Leong
